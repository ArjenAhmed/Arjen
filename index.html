About Me

Electrical Engineering Innovator

I'm an MEng Electrical and Electronic Engineering student at the University of Nottingham, on track for a First Class degree. My passion lies at the intersection of power electronics, embedded systems, and sustainable engineering — building solutions that are both technically rigorous and socially impactful.

Currently an incoming Placement Engineer at GE Vernova within the Power Conversion & Storage UK division, I apply my skills to real-world energy challenges. From designing isolated DC-DC converters to developing peat bog monitoring systems for carbon initiatives, I thrive on end-to-end project ownership — from requirements capture through to PCB layout and validation.

I'm also proud President of the EEE Society, where I lead initiatives connecting students with industry, and contribute to the UoN Racing Formula Student team on safety-critical electrical systems.
